We talk a lot, and he recommended Peronsal Blog to me, I always want a space of my own, so I built a blog based on github+jekyll, and there is nothing in that blog, because my work is on windows system, I encountered many problems when I built my blog, perhapse I was tired, perhapse I had a busy work, I quit the blog.

I thought about the blog the other day suddenly, so I picked up the blog, and I found hexo when I was searching a problem. Hexo my blog

I will learn MarkDown, Git and Hexo to blog my blog, and hello to everyone, wish everyone could live a simple, happy life.
